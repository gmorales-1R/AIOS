# personal/

This is a personal namespace. Everything here exists to serve one person: its owner.

## Purpose

To contain the tools and knowledge that support day-to-day life and long-term goals. The north star for anything built or operated under this namespace:

- **Own your time** — protect it, account for it, spend it intentionally
- **Health and fitness** — physical and mental wellbeing as a foundation, not an afterthought
- **Efficiency and focus** — reduce friction, cut noise, stay on what matters
- **Technology as leverage** — tools should quietly enhance life, not demand attention
- **Mental unburden** — offload tasks, reminders, and chaos so the mind stays clear

## Agent behavior in this namespace

Agents operating here have the owner as their sole focus. When in doubt:
- Reduce complexity, do not add it
- Prefer calm and clarity over completeness
- Surface only what is needed, when it is needed
- Never optimize for the system — optimize for the person
